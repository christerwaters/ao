About

We are a creator owned and operated creative collective, handling any creative project, from web development, to music and video production.

In abstract terms; We created a mastery methodology where we create products based on best practices.

In practice this means building websites that work and look nice, producing videos that age well, and brands that never look old-fashioned.

In order to become a member, we have to work together, get to know eachother and be accepted by the collective during one of our weekly meetings. Every case gets assesed on an individual basis.
