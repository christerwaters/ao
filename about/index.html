About

we are a small Oslo based creative agency, owned and operated by the creators. we have one simple goal, to make creativity relialbe, direct, and datadriven.

We are a non-profit organization built for creators by creators to create a better eco-system for the gig economy. Empowering creative spirits to do what they burn for on their own premises. New members are emitted by firstly coming to one of our meetups, collaborating on a project etc. then they can be voted in or out by the collective.

in practice this means spending just as much time testing as delivering, which means we use more resouces than most on each individual project.
