HOLY

Preamble

Picture yourself walking through Paris on a Sunday early in February, the sun is shining even though it wasent long ago since the first snow hit the ground. Yesterday you were drinking natural wines for the first time, and the experience has left you baffled. You are currently living at a retired vogue photographers house, getting to meet all his cool and artistic friends. They show you natural wine in the evening and share cheese with you in the morning. Just before going on this great adventure you had landed your first real gig as an artist, commisioned to create a cover artwork for an artist named Taylor Bennet.

You eat breakfast, nothing special really, just some crepes and a glass of wine, before you hit the streets of Paris, you walk past monument after monument, it is like you are walking through postcards. Before finally arriving at your destination, Museum Dorsay.
