About

we are a small Oslo based creative agency, owned and operated by the creators. we have one simple goal, to make creativity relialbe, direct, and datadriven.

in theory this means collecting as much data about the reception and use of the designs and solutions we create for our clients then applying that knowledge to our current and future solutions.

in practice this means spending just as much time testing as delivering, which means we use more resouces than most on each individual project.
